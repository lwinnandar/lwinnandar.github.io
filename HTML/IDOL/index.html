Description

Early Life and Education Background

Lee Min-ho was born on June 22, 1987 in Seoul, South Korea. He studied at Konkuk University, where he majored in Film & Art.

Early Life

  • As a child, Lee wanted to be a professional soccer player.
  • He was selected for a youth football class, but an injury in the 5th grade ended his hopes.
  • He continued to follow football and named Nal-do Ho as his favorite player.
  • He has an older sister and his parents.

High School

  • Lee's interest in acting began in his second year of high school.
  • In his senior year, he joined Starhaus Entertainment and began auditioning for roles.

College

  • Lee studied Film & Art at Konkuk University.

Career

Lee Min Ho is a South Korean actor, singer, and model represented by MYM Entertainment. Lee made his acting debut in 2006 in the EBS TV drama Secret Campus. He became popular in Asia for his role in the 2009 drama Boys Over Flowers. He gained fame in Europe for his role in the 2011 drama City Hunter.He became popular in Africa for his role in the 2020 drama The King: Eternal Monarch.In a short span of his acting career, he became a most popular actor worldwide.
